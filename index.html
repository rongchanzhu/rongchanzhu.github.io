About

My research focuses on stochastic partial differential equations (SPDEs), stochastic analysis, mathematical physics and Dirichlet form theory. Currently, I am mainly working on stochastic partial differential equations with singular noise and stochastic fluid equations; stochastic quantization and quantum many body.
